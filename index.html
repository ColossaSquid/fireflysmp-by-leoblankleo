About the Server

Sky Firefly SMP is a scripted Minecraft SMP created by @ArvPlayz7 and @7amoody.buhazza. Like the Unstable SMP — but completely original. Script, map, lore, everything.

★

Scripted Story

An evolving narrative with arcs, characters, and plot twists. Every session moves the story forward.

⚒

Custom Map

A hand-crafted world built specifically for Sky Firefly. Not your average seed.

♥

Teams

Four factions shape the world of Sky Firefly. Choose your allegiance.

Villagers

The Villagers

LEADER: None

Server Rules

Keep the server fun for everyone.

01

Be Respectful

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior.

02

No Griefing

Don't destroy other players' builds unless it's part of the scripted story.

03

No Hacking

Cheating, x-ray, and unfair advantages are banned. This is a scripted SMP, not an anarchy server.

04

Stay In Character

This is a scripted server. Stay true to your team's lore and role during recorded sessions.

05

Follow the Story

Major events are scripted. Don't interfere with story arcs unless you're part of them.

06

Have Fun

At the end of the day, it's a game. Build, fight, and make memories.
